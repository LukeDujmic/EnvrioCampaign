Enviro-Friendly Campaign

Goals

What the Individual Can Do

As an individual, people should try and gear themselves towards accepting policies and decisions that entail favoring the environment. Things as small as properly recycling, avoiding littering, emitting less pollution, and more are always simple options.

Perhaps environment friendliness be better engrained in people's minds if schools from early on had more significant environmental programs, and even offer benefits for participation. Beyond this, workplaces or any public facility for that matter could always try and get a message across for the sake of Earth. Small reminders like these can always spark a sense of duty in people.

What the Higher-ups Could Do

Relating back to what I said earlier about getting a message across, politicians often are the best people in promoting a strong message. Now of course people have their views and this can't happen so easily. This was evident especially with Donald Trump who, according to Sharon Lerner in The Intercept, "has since completed dozens of other environmental rollbacks, including gutting the Clean Water Act . . . rewriting the National Environmental Policy Act". But those who do support the environment should make stronger efforts to get the idea across to the individual.
