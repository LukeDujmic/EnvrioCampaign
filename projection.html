Projection

What if we Neglect The Environment?

Continuing to neglect issues the climate and the environment of this planet will only have dramatic effects on both us AND the planet's entire ecosystem. Average temperatures will only continue to rise, and in turn the ocean level will rise, and certain ecosystems become unsustainable (coral reefs, forests, etc.). Not only that, but increased temperatures have causes and will continue to cause excessive forest fires.

Looking at places like Australia and California this year (2020) truly shows how devasting the already existing effects on the environment have been. So one could only imagine what could happen if this continues.

Focusing on the Environment

If instead humanity puts its efforts towards the environment over other issues, good results can emerge. The time to act on the environment is now. Even with gradual advancements towards environmental research and policy, great strides will be made in the direction a more eco-friendly society.

More research should be put into environment-friendly energy sources such as solar panels. Over the years, more efficient solar panels with reduced costs can eventually make way and be on par with fossil fuels and other sources of energy that emit harmful greenhouse gases. By putting more emphasis on the environment over other social issues, the public eye should open to the significance of this issues and hopefully save this planet from a horrible fate that would only bring chaos upon ourselves.
